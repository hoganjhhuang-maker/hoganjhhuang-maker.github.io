Conference Presentations

From Empire to the Globe: The Translational Evolution of Han Gongqiu and the Changing Images of Xiqu in the West
International Nineteenth Century Studies Association (INCSA) Conference — Revolution, Revelation, Reconciliation
📍 National Union Building, Washington, DC (Online)  ·  21 July 2026

Historiographical analysis tracing two centuries of the translations of Han Gongqiu, examining shifts from assimilative to heterogenized translation practices and the evolving image of Chinese opera in Western contexts.

III International Conference on PhD Research about Translation
📍 Complutense University of Madrid, Spain  ·  12 March 2026
